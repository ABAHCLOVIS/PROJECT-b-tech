Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about HUIB, our portal, and academic life. Click a question to view the answer.

How do I register as a student or lecturer? +
To register, click your role on the home page and follow the registration instructions. For students, use the Student button; for lecturers, use the Lecturer button. Admins have a separate access route.
How do I enroll in a course? +
After logging in as a student, go to the Course Management section and select the courses you wish to enroll in. Follow the prompts to complete your enrollment.
How do I upload assignments or materials? +
Students can upload assignments from their dashboard under the Assignments section. Lecturers can upload materials via the Upload Materials page in their dashboard.
How do I contact my lecturer or classmates? +
You can use the chat feature in your dashboard to communicate with lecturers and classmates, or use the contact information provided in your course details.
Can I access the portal on my mobile device? +
Yes, the HUIB portal is fully responsive and can be accessed on smartphones and tablets as well as desktop computers.
How can I reset my password? +
On the login page, click "Forgot Password?" and follow the instructions to reset your password via your registered email address.
Where can I find course materials and assignments? +
After logging in, navigate to your dashboard. Course materials and assignments are available under the respective sections for students and lecturers.
Who do I contact for technical support? +
For technical support, use the Contact Us section at the bottom of this page or email info@huib.edu.
Is my data secure on the HUIB portal? +
Yes, your data is protected with modern security measures and is only accessible to authorized users.
